Although I'm currently swamped with trying to pass the OSCP I think these are the courses which I've found online that I'd love to attend.
They wouldn't be beginner courses and you'd need to have some background knowledge in IT I would suggest. 

The first one is reasonably priced given the membership options but the other 2 are kinda pricey.
With the SANS one you do get a working PLC to take home though!

This one is provided by OTW (Occupy The Web) who has trained the NSA and is currently taking on Russia with his knowledge. He has written several books on hacking and I'll be attending his course in September. 
With his Member Pro subscription of $36 for a month you can attend the training which is very reasonable. 

* [https://www.hackers-arise.com/scada-security](https://www.hackers-arise.com/scada-security)

You can see him discussing his techniques on David Bombal's channel.

I guess finally it wouldn't be a Cyber Security course post without mentioning probably the most famous Cyber Security course in the world which I'm currently studying for which is the OSCP. 

As part of pen testing ICS you will at times need to access the OT network by first getting domain admin on the Active Directory IT network and pivoting to the OT one. 

I believe these two courses would get you well on your way to achieving that by passing the OSCP and CRTP certifications:

* [https://www.offensive-security.com/pwk-oscp/](https://www.offensive-security.com/pwk-oscp/)
